# Operational KPI Dashboard — Single Source of Truth

**Portfolio recreation** (built on a public sample dataset such as Sample Superstore).

## Business problem
Four business stakeholders calculated the same KPIs differently, producing conflicting reports and no single source of truth.

## BA approach
- Facilitated a definition-alignment workshop; documented each stakeholder's calculation logic.
- Identified the definitional source of each discrepancy and built consensus on one agreed formula per metric.
- Delivered self-service dashboards (Tableau / Power BI) with role-based views and drill-down.

## Result (original engagement)
Single source of truth adopted across all 4 teams within 2 weeks; ~90 minutes/week of analyst time recovered.
